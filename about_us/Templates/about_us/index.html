About Us

Welcome to ClinicMate, your trusted partner in modern healthcare solutions. Launched on December 1, 2023, ClinicMate is dedicated to revolutionizing the way healthcare services are accessed and delivered across Sri Lanka.

Who We Are

ClinicMate is a cutting-edge digital health service provider, designed to offer seamless and convenient medical services directly through our user-friendly mobile app and website. We are committed to bringing healthcare closer to you, ensuring that high-quality medical care is just a few clicks away.

Our Mission

Our mission at ClinicMate is to enhance the healthcare experience for all Sri Lankans by providing a state-of-the-art digital platform that integrates various healthcare services. We aim to simplify the process of accessing medical care, making it more efficient, effective, and accessible to everyone.

Our Services

  • Doctor Appointments: Easily book appointments with a wide network of experienced doctors across numerous specialties.
  • Laboratory Services: Access a range of diagnostic tests and receive your results digitally.
  • Health Records: Securely manage and access your health records anytime, anywhere.

Why Choose ClinicMate?

  • Convenience: Our platform is accessible via our website and mobile app, available on both Android and iOS.
  • Wide Network: Connect with thousands of doctors from leading hospitals and clinics.
  • Comprehensive Care: From booking appointments to getting lab results, we provide a holistic healthcare experience.

Our Vision

We envision ClinicMate as the benchmark for integrated e-commerce healthcare solutions in the Asia-Pacific region. Our goal is to transform the healthcare service sector, setting new standards in digital health solutions and improving the overall health and well-being of our community.

Join Us

At ClinicMate, we believe in the power of technology to enhance healthcare delivery. Join us on our journey to make healthcare more accessible, affordable, and efficient for everyone.
